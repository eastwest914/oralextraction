Affordable Types Oral Surgeon

Tooth Extractions

Simple and surgical tooth extractions are among the most common and affordable oral surgery procedures. They are often recommended for severely damaged, decayed, or problematic teeth.

Wisdom Teeth Removal

Wisdom tooth extraction can help prevent pain, infection, crowding, and other dental complications. Many practices offer affordable treatment plans for wisdom teeth removal.

Impacted Tooth Extraction

Impacted teeth that are trapped beneath the gums or jawbone may require surgical removal to protect oral health and prevent future problems.

Dental Implants

While dental implants are an investment in long-term oral health, many oral surgeons provide financing options to make treatment more affordable.

Why Choose Oral Surgery?

Relieves Pain and Discomfort

Addresses issues such as impacted teeth, infections, and jaw problems that can cause ongoing pain.

Prevents Future Dental Problems

Early surgical treatment can help avoid complications like tooth crowding, gum disease, bone loss, and damage to neighboring teeth.

Improves Oral Function

Restores the ability to chew, speak, and bite properly by correcting structural or dental issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Oral surgery includes procedures performed on the teeth, gums, jaw, and surrounding oral structures. Common treatments include tooth extractions, wisdom teeth removal dental implants, bone grafting, and corrective jaw surgery.
You may need oral surgery if you have impacted teeth, severe tooth decay, jaw problems, facial trauma, gum disease, or require dental implants.
Most oral surgery procedures are performed using local anesthesia, sedation, or general anesthesia to ensure patient comfort. Some soreness and swelling are normal during recovery but can usually be managed with prescribed medications.
